  • home

Profile

He is a Certified Business, Leadership and Life Transformative Coach. A Senior Banker and Business Development Manager and Mentor of over 25years. He is a Human Resource and Relationship Management Consultant. An Industrial sociologist and Senior Lecturer. He is a member of different organizations which embark on human development in different means such as; International Coach Federation (ICF)Nigeria/Global, Enugu Sports Club (Golf Club). A Fellow of Paul Harris Rotary Club, a lover of nature and laws of nature, lover of music (Classical tunes and Highlife), philosophy and life. He is a family Man that is Proudly African and fluent in three languages; English, Igbo and Yoruba.
